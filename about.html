About Whisperwave

Whisperwave is a social platform designed exclusively for university students in South Africa. Our mission is to foster a vibrant and connected student community across different campuses.

Our app allows students to share their thoughts, join communities, and stay informed about campus events. Whether you're looking to make new friends, discuss academic topics, or just stay in the loop, Whisperwave is here to make your university life more engaging and connected.

Founded in 2024, Whisperwave aims to be the go-to platform for South African students seeking a dynamic and interactive online community. We are committed to continuous improvement and look forward to introducing new features that will enhance your campus experience.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Whisperwave is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it. Our Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

We only collect information that is necessary for the functionality of the app, such as your name, email address, and university affiliation. This data is used to create your profile, connect you with other students, and improve your experience on the platform.

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except where required by law. You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time.

For more details, please review our full Privacy Policy.
